faqs

What is this page for?

This page is for anyone who has received a reusable no-boxx return package. It explains how to fill the bag with suitable items, send it back, and support charity and community causes while reducing waste.

What can I send?

Clean, safe, and still-useful items: clothes, shoes, bags, mobile phones, small electronics, accessories, and useful household essentials.

What should I not send?

Please don’t send anything dangerous, broken, dirty, sharp, illegal, or perishable. If you’re unsure about an item, it’s best to leave it out.

Where do the items go?

Once sorted, suitable items are donated to charities and community causes that need them most, so your return helps real people nearby.

Can I reuse the no-boxx package?

Yes — that’s the whole idea. One bag, many returns. The reusable Velcro pouch is built to be used again and again, so nothing goes to waste.

What happens after no-boxx receives my package?

Our team checks and sorts every item, then passes the suitable ones on to charity and community partners. Anything else, we reuse or recycle responsibly.

Every return helps reduce waste and support communities.
